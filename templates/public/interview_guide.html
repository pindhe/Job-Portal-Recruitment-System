Before — Research

Study the company, role and interviewer. Prepare questions and rehearse your key stories out loud.

During — Communicate

Listen carefully, stay concise, and use the STAR method to structure your answers with real examples.

After — Follow up

Send a short thank-you note within 24 hours, reaffirming your interest and a key strength.

The STAR method

A simple framework for answering behavioral questions clearly and convincingly.

S

Situation

Set the context.

T

Task

Your responsibility.

A

Action

What you did.

R

Result

The measurable outcome.

Common interview questions

Give a 60-second pitch: present role, relevant achievements, and why this job is your next step.

Pick a real area you're improving, and explain the concrete steps you're taking to grow.

Connect your skills and goals to the company's mission and the specific role's impact.
