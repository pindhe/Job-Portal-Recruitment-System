Build a CV that gets you hired

A practical, step-by-step guide to writing a modern, ATS-friendly resume that stands out to recruiters and hiring managers.

Check my resume with AI Build my resume

1. Pick a clean structure

Use clear sections: contact, summary, experience, education and skills. Keep it to 1–2 pages.

2. Tailor to the job

Mirror keywords from the job description so your CV passes ATS filters and reaches a human.

3. Show impact, not duties

Start bullets with action verbs and quantify results: “Increased sales by 30%”.

Do

  • Use a professional email and an active phone number
  • Keep formatting consistent and easy to scan
  • Proofread carefully for spelling and grammar
  • Save and send as a PDF unless asked otherwise

Don't

  • Add photos, age or marital status (unless required)
  • Use long paragraphs or dense blocks of text
  • Exaggerate or include false information
  • Use one generic CV for every application
